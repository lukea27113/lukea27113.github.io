Games remade in Scratch

Among Us

Among Us was coded into scratch by scratcher @TimMcCool. How it works (in a nutshell): At the beginning of the game, the project chooses impostors. Those players @me variable is set to Impostor . Currently, there is a button on the remix on the top right corner that changes the @me variable to Impostor . Also hitboxes have been removed.
